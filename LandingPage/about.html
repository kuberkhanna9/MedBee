Our Story

Medbee was born from a simple observation: managing family health records is unnecessarily complicated. Our founders experienced firsthand the challenges of keeping track of medical documents, prescriptions, and health histories across multiple family members.

We set out to create a solution that would make health record management as simple as using a social media app, but with the security and privacy standards that sensitive health data demands.

Today, Medbee is helping thousands of families take control of their health information through our AI-powered platform, making it easier than ever to store, understand, and share important health records.
